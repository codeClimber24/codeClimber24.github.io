About Me

I am a fully qualified and experienced mobile beauty therapist based in Lichfield. I qualified in 2011 and specialise in Calgel, Sienna X, threading and waxing. I am fully insured with Salon Gold.
I work in the Lichfield area, visiting you at home, providing excellent service where you are comfortable. I also serve areas outside of Lichfield, however these clients will be charged a milage fee based on their location, please get in contact with me for more information.
Please feel free to browse my website and get in contact if you have any questions. Booking in advance is advised as last minute appointments cannot always be accommodated. I aim to get back to all calls and messages within 24 hours.
