Events and Prices

We meet every Friday afternoon/evening at Redpoint Climbing Centre in Birmingham. The concessionary price of one session when climbing with us is £6-7 for an adult and £5-6 for a child depending on the time of day. All equipment hire (shoes and harness) is included in the price. We can meet at other times to suit individual needs.

Sessions can be arranged at Redpoint Worcester on Wednesdays by prior appointment.
